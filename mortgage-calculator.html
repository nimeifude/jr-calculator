How mortgage payments are calculated

The calculator uses the standard amortization formula for fixed-rate loans. The loan amount is the home price minus the down payment. A longer term usually lowers the monthly payment but increases total interest.

How to compare mortgage scenarios

Try changing the down payment, rate, and term one at a time. This makes it easier to see which factor has the biggest effect on affordability.
