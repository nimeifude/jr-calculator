Formula

Compound interest: A = P(1 + r/n)nt

Simple interest: A = P(1 + rt)

P is principal, r is annual rate, n is compounding periods per year, and t is years.

How to use the interest calculator

Enter the starting amount, annual percentage rate, and number of years. Choose monthly, quarterly, annual, or daily compounding. If you only want simple interest, select the simple interest option.

When compound interest matters

Compounding becomes more important over longer time periods. Even small rate changes can produce a large difference when interest is reinvested and earns additional interest.
