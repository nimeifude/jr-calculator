Planning note

Future savings show the nominal account value. Today's value adjusts that result for inflation to estimate purchasing power.

Returns are simplified and do not include taxes, account fees, or market volatility.

How retirement savings projections work

The calculator compounds the current savings and adds monthly contributions over the full time horizon. Inflation is then used to estimate what the projected balance may feel like in today's dollars.

How to improve a retirement projection

Compare several annual return and inflation assumptions. Also test contribution increases, later retirement dates, or larger starting balances to see how each lever affects the result.
